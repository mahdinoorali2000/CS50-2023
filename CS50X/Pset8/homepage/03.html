The desire of computers to see :


Computer vision is different from image processing. Image processing is the process of creating a new image from an existing image, which generally includes simplifying or improving the content, and is a type of digital signal processing and is not related to understanding the content of the image.

The computer vision system may need image processing algorithms to initially examine the images and perform actions to improve its performance on the images, such as:


  • Normalizing the photometric features of the photo such as light and color.
  • Cropping the edges of the image like centering an object in the photo
  • Remove digital noise from the photo such as digital noise at low light levels
